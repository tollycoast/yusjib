Medical Director



Dr Yusuf Abdulraheem, MPH, FCPM, FIPMA,FCPM,HCILRM, HFIIM

is a renowned Physician and Public Health Specialist with special interest in Industrial Medicine. The Medical Director, Yusjib Industrial Medicare is an astute professional manager across different managerial fields and able to demonstrate strong clinical expertise with proven ability to examine, investigate, diagnose and treat patients.
Dr. Abdulraheem is an icon of academic excellence with the following certificates of professionalism:

  • MBBS, MPH holder of University of Ilorin
  • Fellow Institute of Proffessional managers and Administrators of Nigeria (FIPMA)
  • Honorary Fellow Institute of Information Management, Africa (HFIIM)
  • Honorary Fellow Institute of Loans and Risks Management of Nigeria (HCILRM)
  • Fellow of Chartered Institute of Public Management of Nigeria (FCPM)

Dr. Yusuf Abdulraheem as the MD of Yusjib Industrial Medicare®

  • provides professional and effective leadership and management of patients and to hospitals
  • facilitates side by side coaching and professional development in the areas of medical skills, preventive care, associate engagement and client experience.
  • conducts formal review of each doctor on an annual basis, address and correct any issues affecting quality of medicine
  • cultivates a principled culture within each hospital and the market through exemplary leadership.
  • commits to and support medical teams in exceeding measurable practice goals, support business goals and hold teams accountable through effective communication, encouragement and recognition.
