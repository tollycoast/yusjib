Overview

Yusjib Industrial Medicare® is a World affirmed Hospital that is positioned and rendering medical management in all area of medical practice. This is made possible with an optimum commitment of our specialized medical practitioners. The hospital is also a National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS) accredited facility in both Primary and Secondary categories (Surgery, Internal Medicine, Paediatric and Child Health as well as Obstetrics and Gynaecology).
Since the hospital's inception, our building blocks has remained financial discipline and service excellence. The hospital's senior management team has long understood that these two objectives need not be mutually exclusive.
As an organization, our long-term success depends on the ability to translate our commitment of affordable and accessible healthcare into real change. We have four principles as our guide as we strive to provide exceptional value for members, employers, and healthcare service providers:

  • Easy and Simple Experience
    Everyone at YUSJIB Industrial Medicare is uncompromising in their commitment to ensure that all our patients have an easy, simple, and productive experience whether registering as a new patient, or seeking for continuous medicare.
  • Operational Excellence
    We pay fanatical attention to operational excellence, continually refining advanced platforms and processes that are essential to what we do - delivering a growing range of services in the most efficient and cost-effective way possible to an even larger group of people.
  • Financial Discipline
    At every level, we guard our financial resources conscientiously, knowing that financial discipline provides the foundation upon which we can deliver products and services with utmost value.
  • Empowering Environment
    Underlying the performance in each of these areas is our belief that taking exceptionally trained professionals (Medical Doctors, Pharmacists, Nurses/Mid-wives, laboratory Technologists,etc) placing them in an empowering environment, and holding them accountable for their efforts in the best way to exceed expectations in whatever we do.

Our Mission

"To contribute to the development of the African Continent through the provision of quality healthcare products and services that enhance basic living and consistently uphold our stakeholder's interest in the industrial world"

Vision

"To become the pace setter in Africa's health care delivery by providing world class products and services to individuals, families and corporate bodies."

Core Values

  • We work as a team.
