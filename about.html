About Us


YUSJIB Industrial Medicare is a global leader in clinical diagnosis and management, healthcare professionals in industrial medicine and point of care services with the vital information required therein, treat and monitor patients and corporate bodies.
We offer comprehensive solutions designed to improve clinical outcome, streamline workflow and enhance operational efficiency of patients and corporate organizations.
At YUSJIB Industrial Medicare, we are driven to ensure that individuals, organizations and corporate world we serve receive the greatest possible value for their healthcare investment. We do this by bringing together medical professionals in different fields of medicine and administration, making the best available possible information and devising solutions that help people enjoy optimum health.
Establishment of an Industrial sick bay within establishments or management of such clinic if it is already in existence. Carrying out pre-employment, pre-placement, periodic and promotional medical checkup of employees as well as their employers.
Carry out a walk-through survey of the company to identify equipment or machines that might cause injury to their users, and periodic health talk and health-related screening exercises.
We are committed to delivering these products and services to an ever-widening base of companies and industries through clinical diagnosis and optimal care. YUSJIB Industrial Hospital has the expertise, the experience, and the ability to craft new diagnostic and curative procedures (plus modern laboratory equipments), new processes, and new services needed to make healthcare more accessible and affordable to all.

Our Mission

Vision
