Service

YUSJIB Industrial Medicare provides a full range of products and services, including the followings;

Clinical Diagnosis and Company Management

Backed by quality, affordability and thousands of certified medical professionals, we embark on treatment of both minor and major ailments that could affect company's staff and their dependants according to company policies at our World class registered hospital. We also provide specialist care in all disciplines of medicine ranging from General and Specialised Surgeries, Internal medicine, Paediatric and Child Health, Obsterics & Gynaecology, Ophthamology, Dental and other sub- specialities.

Establishment of Industrial Sick Bay (Clinic) within the Company

Due to our extensive and advanced knowledge on Industrial medicine, we intend to help companies and industries to set up a modern clinic within the company and sustain such with appropriate personnel ranging from excellent nursing staff, environmentalist, hygienist and provision of first aid equipments and medications.

Medical Examinations (Check-up)

With varying degree of both communicable and non-communicable diseases affecting human especially those within working age group, we intend to help individuals and corporate bodies carry out the following medical checkup; pre-employment, pre-placement, periodic, pre-admission, pre-insurance and promotional test for their intending staff (those being screened or considering for employment) and even their employee for an optimally safe working and family environment. This will enables employers (companies and industries) to have their employees' medical conditions detailed and documented in their files to curtail future possible legal litigation due to work hazards.

Walk through Survey of the Company

"Prevention is said to be safer and cheaper". YUSJIB Industrial Medicare, a pioneer industrial health care provider offers to carry out company diagnosis through the power of physical examinations as well as mechanical, behavioral or biochemical analysis of industrial environment, effluents and or even both light and heavy equipments assessment in order to detect any malfunctioning ones early and replace, repair or treat such hazards as early as possible. We also engage in the provision of protective materials and gadgets against specific job requirements. These include, Face masks, Goggle, Laboratory clothes, Ear plugs/muff et cetera.

Health Talk and Health Related Conditions Screening Services

With our teaming staff and collaboration with different specialists in the field of medicine, YUSJIB Industrial Medicare also carry out quarterly health talk on major medical conditions peculiar to the Industrial medicine and even outside Industrial medicine. We also carry out screening of both minor and major medical conditions ranging from Systemic Hypertensions, Diabetes Mellitus, Hepatitis B infection, Chronic liver disease, Cataract, Glaucoma, Tuberculosis and even Malaria parasites.

Behavioral Health

YUSJIB Industrial Medicare's Behavioral Health Business, is home of The Total Behavioral Solution - dedicated to improving the wellbeing of our members through a quality, clinical outcome-oriented model. We provide innovative behavioral health and work-life services for health plans, employer groups, and public program.
